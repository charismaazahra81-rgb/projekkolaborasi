Plastic Waste Observation 1

Waste is the remaining material that comes from the activities of living things, which no longer has use value and is usually discarded. Waste can be in the form of solid, liquid, or gas. If not properly managed, it can cause environmental pollution. On this occasion, I will explain the results of my observation about plastic waste.


In general, plastic waste is a serious environmental problem because it is difficult to decompose. Plastic has many types, such as food packaging plastic, plastic bottles, plastic straws, and many more. Each type of plastic waste has different physical characteristics, which affect how it is processed and reused.


In this observation, I observed plastic food packaging waste. This plastic comes from snack wrappers, instant noodle packs, coffee sachets, and shopping bags. The texture is flexible but difficult to break down in nature, so it needs to be reprocessed to avoid polluting the environment. Usually, plastic has various types and colors, from clear to bright, and is thin and easy to cut.


From my observation results, food packaging plastic waste can be turned into beautiful and artistic collages. Through processes such as sorting, cleaning, cutting, and sticking, plastic that was once useless can be transformed into beautiful works of art. This activity not only helps reduce pollution but also provides opportunities for creativity and produces products with economic value.


Plastic Waste Observation 2

Plastic waste is one of the most serious environmental problems in Indonesia. The increasing use of plastic in daily life has led to a rise in the amount of plastic waste that does not decompose properly, causing negative impacts on the environment and human life.

Plastic waste found in the surrounding environment comes in various forms and types. Food and beverage packaging is the most commonly found type of plastic waste. In addition, plastic bags, plastic wrapping, and other plastic items are also frequently discovered. This plastic waste can come from various sources, such as households, industries, and traditional markets.

Plastic waste that is not properly managed can cause serious environmental damage. It can contaminate water and soil, as well as negatively affect the lives of humans and animals. Therefore, efforts are needed to reduce plastic use and increase public awareness of the importance of proper plastic waste management in order to minimize its negative impact on the environment.

reducing plastic use and managing plastic waste properly, we can help reduce the negative effects of plastic waste on the environment. Other benefits include reducing water, soil, and air pollution, as well as protecting the lives of marine and terrestrial animals. In addition, it can lower waste management costs and increase public awareness about the importance of protecting the environment.


Plastic Waste Observation 3

Waste is leftover material or waste produced from human activities that is no longer wanted. This time, I will explain about plastic waste. Waste can be harmful to us and cause negative impacts on our health.


Plastic waste is the result of discarded plastic items from humans, such as detergent packaging, snack wrappers, bread wrappers, and others. We can sort plastic waste that is solid and dry. Plastic waste comes from various sources and has a long durability. Plastic waste from snacks can actually be useful for all of us. However, plastic waste that is left unattended can cause soil pollution or even lead to flooding because plastic cannot decompose on its own. Therefore, we must be creative in making useful items from plastic waste so that the amount of plastic around us can be reduced.


If we reuse plastic waste, we can create something useful. From plastic waste, we can make products that have artistic and functional value. Examples include pencil holders, plant pots, and tissue containers. From these activities, we can protect nature by reducing environmental pollution, preventing floods, and even processing waste into simple compost or fertilizer.
